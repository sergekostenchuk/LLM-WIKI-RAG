# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Be respectful, specific, and constructive. Welcome people with different experience levels and backgrounds. Critique ideas and implementations without attacking people. Do not publish private information, credentials, or confidential source material.

Harassment, intimidation, discriminatory language, sexualized conduct, sustained disruption, and deliberate disclosure of another person's private information are unacceptable.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository maintainer through the contact methods on the maintainer's GitHub profile. The maintainer may edit or remove contributions, restrict participation, or ban participants when necessary to protect the community.

This compact policy is inspired by common open-source community standards and applies to project spaces and project-related public representation.
